
I downloaded the certs from: https://www.googleapis.com/oauth2/v1/certs

I converted the certs from x509 to public keys in PKCS#8

(cert was on the form -----BEGIN CERTIFICATE-----)

openssl x509 -in ./cert1 -noout -pubkey

(this gave me something like -----BEGIN PUBLIC KEY-----)
